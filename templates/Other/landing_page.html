About

At Ocean Of Notions Performance company, we specialize in creating innovative school workshop ideas tailored to a variety of educational needs. Our Masterworks program offers dynamic workshops designed for the Junior Cert and Leaving Certificate students, providing them with valuable learning experiences that extend beyond the traditional classroom. Our workshops cover a range of subjects including Shakespearean classics, comparison studies for the Senior Cycle, and film studies for all ages. We pride ourselves on developing engaging and educational experiences that meet the specific needs of both junior and senior cycle students.
Masterworks is spearheaded by our founder Judie Charmers, a performer, director, producer, and most importantly, a dedicated teacher. With extensive training in Shakespearean classics at Themes University’s Royal College of Music and Media, Judie combines her experience with a passion for literature. She expertly balances structure with flexibility, making her workshops ideal for both the Junior Cert and the Leaving Certificate. Over the past X years, Judie has become a familiar face in English classrooms across the country. Her expertise in Shakespearean classics, film, and overall story structure ensures that her workshops leave a lasting impression on students, far beyond the confines of the classroom.
